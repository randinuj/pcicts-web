History of President's College Information & Communication Technology Society

In 2016, the President’s College Information Technology Society (PCITS) was established with a vision to introduce the world of technology to the students of President’s College. The first steps were taken by Mr. Navoda Kaveesha, the founding Chairman, who laid the foundation for the society. Under the unwavering guidance of Ms. Upeksha Priyarangika, the Teacher-in-Charge, PCITS was designed to be a platform for students eager to explore the growing field of Information Technology.

As the years passed, the society evolved. In 2020, it was reformed under the guidance of Mr. Sashi Wijethunga as the Teacher-in-charge. Despite the challenges of the pandemic, the society spearheaded impactful projects such as the school’s Online Learning Management System, the first-ever Virtual Vesak Zone & Creative Competition in the area, and several other virtual initiatives, marking a new chapter in its journey.

In 2022, the club was integrated into the President’s College Technological Society as the ICT Unit. Chaired by Mr. Nethum Santhisa & Mr. Malitha Mihiranga under the guidance of Mr. Sampath Nilangarathne as Master-in-Charge, the unit quickly gained prominence. This period was marked by Revolution'22, the first-ever intra-school innovation competition and exhibition organized by the ICT Unit and active participation in innovation competitions at various levels.

After more than a year of silence, a turning point came in 2024 when the ICT Unit was reformed as a standalone society and was rebranded as the President's College Information & Communication Technology Society (PCICTS), this time with a renewed vision for the future honoring the Presidents' legacy. With only a few members at the start, it quickly grew in influence under the leadership of Randinu Jayaratne as Chairman and Ms. Upeksha Priyarangika as Teacher-in-Charge. In 2024/25, the society took part in prestigious ICT events, further cementing its role as a key player in the local ICT landscape.

Later, the society received official recognition with a growing membership across grades 10 to 13, the society firmly established itself as the symbol of technological excellence within the school.

Today, the President’s College Information & Communication Technology Society (PCICTS) stands as a testament to the vision of its founders. From its humble beginnings to its current status as a dynamic force in the world of ICT, the society continues to inspire the next generation of innovators, equipped with the tools and knowledge to shape the future.
