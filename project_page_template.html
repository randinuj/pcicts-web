About NovaQuest

NovaQuest is President's College ICT Society's flagship annual quiz competition, designed to test and celebrate students' knowledge in information and communication technology. This exciting event brings together the brightest minds from different grades to compete in a series of challenging rounds covering various ICT domains.

From programming fundamentals to emerging technologies like AI and blockchain, NovaQuest covers the full spectrum of modern ICT knowledge. Participants will face questions ranging from basic computer literacy to advanced technical concepts, making it an inclusive yet challenging competition for all skill levels.

Rules and Regulations

Eligibility

  • Open to all current students of President's College
  • Participants must be in grades 9-13
  • Valid student ID required for participation
  • Registration fee must be paid before the deadline

Competition Format

  • Round 1: Written preliminary round (50 questions, 45 minutes)
  • Round 2: Semi-final buzzer round (Top 20 participants)
  • Round 3: Final showdown (Top 6 participants)
  • Each round will have increasing difficulty levels

Question Categories

  • Computer Hardware and Software
  • Programming and Software Development
  • Internet and Web Technologies
  • Database Management Systems
  • Cybersecurity and Data Protection
  • Emerging Technologies (AI, IoT, Blockchain)

Important Rules

  • No electronic devices allowed during the competition
  • Participants must arrive 30 minutes before the event
  • Any form of cheating will result in immediate disqualification
  • Decisions of the judging panel are final
  • Late arrivals will not be accommodated
