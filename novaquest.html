About NovaQuest

NovaQuest is President's College ICT Society's flagship annual quiz competition started in 2025, designed for students from grades 9 to 11 to test and celebrate their knowledge in information and communication technology. This exciting event brings together the brightest minds from different grades to compete in a series of challenging rounds covering various ICT domains.

From programming fundamentals to emerging technologies like AI and blockchain, NovaQuest covers the full spectrum of modern ICT knowledge. Participants will face questions ranging from basic computer literacy to advanced technical concepts, making it an inclusive yet challenging competition for all skill levels.
